Pressure Control (2015)

As part of one of the courses in first year of university, my team and I had to figure out a way to avoid delivery of soggy food. I was the leader of the group and managed to initiate this idea. We ended up pitching this idea a final product.


More Information about the design

The design consists of humidity sensor and heat sensor. The food is packaged into regular packaging system with holes on top to resolve the moisture issue. The container contains an open air filter cardboard on top (Figure 2.1.B) that allow air to escape from the system. The container is programmed such that when the humidity sensors detects certain humidity level, top lids of the container open up to allow moisture to escape. It has a portable heating pan at the bottom. The heating pan is managed by the heat sensors that maintain the internal and surrounding temperature. This allows safe environment for system in the trunk of a car and food is not burned.

The Chipmunk Village (2016)

Out client wanted to develop a Chipmunk Game that is entertaining for both Chipmunks and audience to watch. I lead the team and initiated the following idea. This is the end product of our engineering project.


More Information about the design

Chipmunk Village is a game that is specifically built to allow domestic Chipmunks to play and grab food. The design includes three containers; each has certain tasks for Chipmunks to perform. The design enables the Chipmunks to go through certain obstacles and finally attain food. Container number one and three provide means of entrance/exit. The second container is closed from all sides and contains food for the Chipmunks. The design is made such that each container can only accommodate one Chipmunk in each container. The exterior frame is made of plexiglass which is durable and transparent, so it can allow users to watch Chipmunks play from all angles. The interior part of design contains a park with artificial grass. Each item is put in place with Velcro. This makes it easy for the user to take apart any items whenever needed. Also, it allows users to place new items in the game easily. The design contains pipeline to allow Chipmunks to go from one container to the other.
